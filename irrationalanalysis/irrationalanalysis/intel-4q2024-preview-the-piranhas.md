Problems I have with the leading investment bank model:

  1. Having non-AMD + non-INTC market share as flat.

    1. Historically, TAM - Intel - AMD = Apple.

    2. Apple is going to grow…

    3. Nvidia and Qualcomm both go from 0% to something. 

  2. Intel re-gains market share over the next two quarters then becomes stable? 

    1. Really guys?

    2. From product quality alone, AMD is clearly going to gain meaningful share over the next two years.




There is no way Intel has stable market share in desktop. Where are these numbers coming from? Even if you have AMD as flat market share, Apple + Nvidia + Qualcomm are going to at least get 2-5% incremental market share by 4Q2026.
