Dielectric cores of packages have standard thicknesses, EM propagation coefficients, and design rules.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!tsIl!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fa3b38c13-7741-4fc7-a304-8803f7e46183_515x224.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!tsIl!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fa3b38c13-7741-4fc7-a304-8803f7e46183_515x224.png)

There is a massive packaging industry built around **standard practices.**

  * EDA tool flows.

  * Core and Pre-Preg suppliers

  * Simulation databases of materials at various standard thicknesses.

  * Packaging/Substrate Manufactures

  * Packaging Houses (the company that puts chip onto package)




[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!R_wZ!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F5fd56850-f79d-4940-abba-91a8baff0c81_1005x1008.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!R_wZ!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F5fd56850-f79d-4940-abba-91a8baff0c81_1005x1008.png)Public datasheet of a laminated dielectric available for advanced PCB and package design.
