So to recap, liquified natural gas…

  1. Requires unusually expensive and specialized ships.

  2. Requires expensive, specialized, difficult to build/repair preparation terminals.

  3. Has far fewer producers than oil.

  4. Has several countries (Japan, South Korea, China, <large portion of EU>) dependent on it due to heating, an inelastic demand.
