Marvell is up 20% today while everything else in semis/optics is down around 10%. Some of this move is a short squeeze.

Had some puts against Marvell and those went to zero. Given that Lumentum is my largest position and (presumably) a lot of funds were long LITE, short MRVL, I guess Matt Murphy fucked my portfolio twice today.

Congrats buddy. You managed to tie SP500 in terms of 5-year performance during the greatest semiconductor cycle of all time.
