Anyway… what does this have to do with Astera Labs?

Facts:

  1. The vast majority of ALAB revenue is from PCIe re-timer chips. (> 95%)

  2. By far, the two largest customers are [NVDA 0.00%↑](https://substack.com/discover/stocks/NVDA) and [AMZN 0.00%↑](https://substack.com/discover/stocks/AMZN)

    1. Amazon has purchase-volume based warrants which incentivizes them to single-source.

    2. Nvidia does not want to prop up AI semi-custom ASIC vendors such as [AVGO 0.00%↑](https://substack.com/discover/stocks/AVGO) and [MRVL 0.00%↑](https://substack.com/discover/stocks/MRVL) so there is a strong incentive to continue single-sourcing.

  3. ALAB has **ZERO CONTENT** in GB200 NVL systems.

Re-timers extend reach and help with loss (how long the channel is) and reflections (how many connectors, medium changes, …) there are. **A short, simple channel that only passes through a single PCB (no backplane, no extra connectors/cables) does not need re-timing.**

Astera Lab’s revenue from their largest customer is about to get cut in half. The evidence is in plain sight.

This ticker is an attractive short candidate. Unfortunately, I cannot participate because of ethical reasons.
